Pizza Recipe

How to make an authentic Italian pizza

Ingredients

  • 500g Wheatflour
  • 250g of lukewarm water
  • 5g of active yeast
  • 1 Tablespoon salt
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • a bit of Oil
  • 200ml of tomato sauce
  • preferred Toppings

How to make it

  1. Mix the water, yeast, sugar and salt and let it sit for 15min
  2. Add the mixture to the flour and add the Oil
  3. Knead until a dough is formed
  4. Spread the dough and put the tomato sauce on it
  5. Top it with your preferred toppings
  6. bake at 300Celsius until golden brown
