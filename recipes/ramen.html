Ramen Recipe

How to make traditional japanese Ramen

Ingredients

  • miso
  • Sesame oil
  • chicken broth
  • white pepper
  • Ramen noodles (fresh or dried)
  • 200g pork
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g bean sprouts
  • 1 green onion

How to make it

  1. bring 1L of chicken broth to simmer and add the miso, sesame oil and the white pepper
  2. next braise the pork in a different pan, and cook the 2 eggs softboiled
  3. Add your cooked noodles in a big bowl
  4. Add your broth
  5. Add yout toppings
  6. Decorate it with green onions
  7. Enjoy!
