Kebab Recipe

How to make the best homemade Kebabs you have ever made

Ingredients

  • 400g of Beef or Mutton (minced)
  • 20g cumin
  • 15g garlic
  • 1 Onion
  • Various Spices(personal preference)

How to make

  1. Add the various spices to the Meat and knead it
  2. After thorough kneading pack the meat in the freezer for 4 hours
  3. Cut the frozen meat in thin slices
  4. heat up a pan to high heat and add butter
  5. cook the slices until they are done and a bit charred
  6. eat it like a sandwich or with rice
